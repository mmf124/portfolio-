Field of study

Nutrition Science

I am studying Nutrition Science with a focus on how food, health, and communication intersect. My coursework spans dietetics, biochemistry, and public health — preparing me for a career where I can translate complex nutritional research into clear, actionable guidance for diverse audiences. I am particularly interested in community nutrition, food access equity, and health promotion.

Diversity statement

My commitment

I believe that health and nutrition should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background, culture, or socioeconomic status. Throughout my studies and professional work, I am committed to centering equity — acknowledging that systemic barriers shape who has access to nutritious food and quality healthcare. I strive to communicate in ways that are culturally responsive, inclusive, and free of judgment, and I am dedicated to advocating for food justice in every space I occupy. I approach every project and collaboration with a commitment to listening, learning, and amplifying underrepresented voices in the health and nutrition fields.
